Turning furry monster:

Coloring the paper cup with all kinds of colors you like and punching a hole and inserting the straw into the cup. Curling the string adding two eyes to make the monster and put it on the bottom side of the cup. Now you blow the straw with the air and sound. The sound vibration makes the little furry monster turn and turn.

Floating Gold Fish🐠(Oil-Water Separation Theory)

1. Draw a little gold fish with your color markers on the back of your silver spoons and wait till the ink dry for a few seconds.

2. Immerse your spoons into the water then you will see gold fish floating on it.

Rainbow Bridge( Capillary Theory)

1. Mark three different colors( Red, Blue, and Black) on the tissue and put a few dots about three centimeters away from the color marks and look which color runs the fastest.( The correct answer is Red. The reason why the colors seems to be able to run fast is there are many pores on the surface of the tissue which absorb liquid in no time.)

2. Putting paper towels on the top of three different colors liquid and you will see them and turning into beautiful paper towels.

Making super jelly balls(not for eating!!!

1. You pour 4 grams of sodium alginate into the hot water and stir them evenly.
2. You also need to prepare three different kinds of color drinks such like apple juice, grape juice or vegetable juice.
3. Adding chloride calcium into #2 each individually and mix them all.
4. Using dropper and adding a few drops of #3 into #1 and you will see chunky jellies floating on the top.
